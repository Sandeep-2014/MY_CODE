Pardeep Narwal

Pardeep Narwal, an eminent kabaddi player, gained fame with the Patna Pirates in the Pro Kabaddi League. Born on February 16, 1997, in Haryana, India, he is celebrated for his exceptional raiding prowess, holding records for most raid points in a PKL season.
